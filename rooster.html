Personality

Roosters are active people that like to stand out in a crowd with their good humour and entertaining nature. Their often very honest and forthcoming, often becoming boastful and arrogant as a result. They like to hold the attention of others at all times, and can often be found talking to do just that.

Health & Careers

Roosters are very healthy people overall. They are active, getting a good amount of exercise, and are able to fend off illness well. Stress can get to roosters, and they may feel moody at times because of it.

Roosters are quite motivated, perhaps the most so of any zodiac animal. They tend to put career first, and are hardworking individuals that can adapt to any circumstance. For these reasons, good careers for roosters include:

  • Dentist
  • Firefighter
  • Sales person
  • Soldier
  • Surgeon
  • Waiter

Relationships

Roosters are loyal people, and are devoted to their friends and significant other. They keep to their promises, but are sometimes braggarts, boasting about their accomplishments to others.
