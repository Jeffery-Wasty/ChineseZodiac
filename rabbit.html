Personality

Rabbits are very gentle, elegant people. They are often kind towards others, and patient in other's attempts at socializing. They may be seen as aloof at times, and perhaps stubborn, but overall are easy to relate to socially. They are faithful to those around them, but perhaps too withdrawn at times. Their cautios nature sometimes leads to them missing oppurtunities.

Health & Careers

Rabbits have average health overall. They tend to have more flus and colds during the winter months, when compared to other animals. This can be chalked up to a poor diet and lack of exercise. Overall they should try to drink more water, and moisturize to relive skin problems.

Rabbits are usually scholarly and well read, and are able to make workplace relationships fairly well. They love a career where they can earn a good pay, and are also fairly skilled with their hands. For these reasons, good careers for rabbits include:

  • Breeder
  • Doctor
  • Educator
  • Police officer
  • Politician
  • Priest/Nun

Relationships

With rabbits, you don't need to work very hard to build a reationship with them, however you might need to help them overcome their weaknesses. Rabbits are very social. They have a nice sense of humor, converse well, and possess a gentle temperment. However their kind nature may be their downfall at times as rabbits are easily swayed emotionally.
