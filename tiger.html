Personality

Tigers are very forward people often described as brave and confident. These traits are welcomed by some, and less than welcomed by others, but the opinions of others do not easily sway tigers as they always have enrgy and drive. They may be hard to work with because of their stubborn and contorlling attitude, but one only needs to give the tiger the illusion of being in control to keep them happy.

Health & Careers

Tigers are active people who rarely get sick. They may be a bit reckless in their activities at times though, leading to injury. Even though tigers exhibit copious amounts of energy, they should find time to relax, as their body can not always keep up with their spirit.

As tigers like to command the top role, jobs where they can be in charge are the best for them. Equally jobs where they are the center of attention, or respected by others, will do well for a tiger's ego. For these reasons, good careers for Tigers include:

  • Actor
  • Advertising agent
  • Artist
  • Musician
  • Pilot
  • Writer

Relationships

Tigers like to be in charge of their relationships. They find it hard to trust others and believe in their own confidence above all else. This leads to trouble communicating with others, and fewer friends overall. They may know a lot of people, but may not be close with any of them. In matters of love, tigers are not romantic. They strongly value a partner who feels as adventurous as they do.
