Personality

Pigs are seen as compassionate and generous people. They like to help others, and will avoid seeking out hekp, unless absolutely needed. They handle problems in a calm manner, and feel that no matter what the challenge, they can overcome it.

Health & Careers

Pigs generally have good health throughout life, if they can keep consumption in moderation. Exercise is something that pigs avoid in general, so they must keep special mind of it to live a long life. They usually know how to relax, and have few sleep problems.

Pigs often have good luck in their careers and will ask help of others, when they need it. They are often very creative, and have good imagination. For these reasons, good careers for pigs include:

  • Caterer
  • Doctor
  • Entertainer
  • Interior designer
  • Sales clerk
  • Veterinarian

Relationships

Pigs enjoy social relationships, and will actively seek them out, maing them easy to befriend. They make good friends as they avoid getting caught up in the little things that may ruin other sign's friendships. In matters of love, pigs are very loving and affectionate. They like to be in long term relationships, but may not handle problems in the relationship very well.
