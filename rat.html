Personality

With their sharp personality and keen eye, rats can often guage new situations and adapt quickly. They are quick to take advantage of new opputunities, and often create their own as their curiosity drives them forward. They are kind, but sometimes impolite, courageous, but not good leaders, and are always surrounded by friends.

Health & Careers

Rats are healthy overall, mostly due to their excess of energy which drives them towards exercise. Rats should watch their diet, as their busy schedule may lead to them avoiding a healthy diet, and should also make sure they are properly hydrated.

Rats are quite perspective and able to pick out small details. They also exercise good judgement when it comes to difficult situations. They may be cautios at times, but tend to be serious in their work endevours. For these reasons, good careers for rats include:

  • Administrator
  • Broadcaster
  • Director
  • Entrepreneur
  • Lawyer
  • Writer

Relationships

Rats enjoy an active lifestyle with a flexible social circle. They like to diversify their events, and are more interested in quantity over quality when it comes to friends. They can be easy to get into a relationship with, but also easy to break up with, as their inability to deepen relationships extends to love as well.
