Personality

Snakes are sharp, wise, and exhibit good intuition. They often act according to their own judgement, shunning advice from others, and prfer to work alone in general. They are normally introverted, and will become even more so when stressed.

Health & Careers

Snakes like to avoid loud and busy environments, which helps to lower stress. They need more relaxation time when compared to other animals, and may need to take a vacation to achieve this.

Snakes are creative and hardworking individuals. They may try many different jobs over the course of their life, as they often become bored working in one setting. They are great problem solvers, and work well with tight deadlines. For these reasons, good careers for snakes include:

  • Analyst
  • Dietician
  • Investigator
  • Painter
  • Potter
  • Scientist

Relationships

Snakes are not able to make friends easily. This is due to their withdrawn and choosy nature, that leaves them unable to open up to others. They become much closer to someone once that person has earned their trust, and this closeness may border on obsession at times.
