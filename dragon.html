Personality

Dragons are considered to be confident, intelligent, enthusiastic, and possessing a sharp tongue. They are always ready to take up challenges, regardless of the risks. This confidence may sometimes be seens as aggressiveness, leading dragons to often be associated with critisism.

Health & Careers

Since dragons are hardworking, they tend to live very healthy lives, however overworking may cause them stress, and stress-related health issues. Dragons in particular should look out for problems related to the liver, gall bladder, intestines, stomach, and skin.

Dragons are always up for a challenge, and enjoy careers where they are offered a challenge on a daily basis. For these reasons, good careers for dragons include:

  • Broker
  • Engineer
  • Inventor
  • Journalist
  • Sales person
  • Teacher

Relationships

Dragons may hesitiate when it comes to commitment, especially in a relationship. Howver they approach each relatioship as true love are are willing to do whatever it takes to make it last. For friendships, they tend to be very warm, and very generous, friends.
