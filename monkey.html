Personality

Monkeys are people with charm, wit, and intelligence. They tend to exhibit traits associated with actual monkeys, such as a tendency for mischievousness. They love playing practical jokes, which may not always be to the favour of others. They are fast learners, and quick to bite on oppurtunites, and in general prefer to fast-paced urban lifestyle over a rural one.

Health & Careers

Since monkeys have an active lifestyle, they are often very healthy. Any illness they may experience will be for psychological reasons. Monkeys need to remember to take breaks sometimes, and conserve energy. As monkeys are usually outdoors, they should also try to be extra cautious.

Monkeys work very hard over the course of their careers, and are quick to adapt to difference situations. For these reasons, good careers for Monkeys include:

  • Accountant
  • Banker
  • Director
  • Engineer
  • Scientist
  • Stock trader

Relationships

In general, monkeys are very sociable people that are easy to get to know and to relate to. Monkeys take their time settling down in a relationship. The often like to fool around, and may get bored with a partner before they really get to know them. Once they find that special someone though, they are entirely loyal to them.
